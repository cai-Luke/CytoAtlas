About CytoAtlas
CytoAtlas is a curated virtual slide reference of body fluid cytospin preparations captured with BenchVision, a native Android microscopy app built for hematology lab work. Each case is a registered, stitched composite of multiple microscope fields captured through a Samsung Galaxy S25 Ultra mounted on a clinical microscope via a universal clip adapter.
How to Use
Select a case from the sidebar. Use your mouse wheel or pinch gesture to zoom into the composite slide. Numbered markers highlight regions of diagnostic interest — click a marker or its description to navigate between the two. Filter by specimen type using the pills in the header.
Technology
Composites are stitched from individually captured tiles using ORB feature matching, pose-graph least-squares alignment, and minimum-cost seam routing. The viewer is powered by OpenSeadragon.
