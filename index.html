About the project

CytoAtlas is a companion to HemeAtlas, built with BenchVision — a native Android microscopy app for body fluid cell survey at 50× and 100× oil immersion. Cases are cytospin preparations stained with Wright-Giemsa, photographed through a Samsung Galaxy S25 Ultra on a clinical microscope at Northwell Health.

Regions of interest are annotated by AI draft and reviewed by a hematology professional. The "AI-generated draft" badge disappears once pathologist commentary is confirmed.

Technical stack

Composites are stitched on-desktop using ORB feature matching and pose-graph least-squares alignment. The viewer runs on OpenSeadragon 4.1.1 with fractional-coordinate annotation overlays. Capture runs on Android via CameraX with normalized circle-crop geometry across objectives.
